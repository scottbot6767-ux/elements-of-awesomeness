◆ About the Program ◆
01
The Foundation
The Four Elements
In the beginning, ancient peoples believed everything in the universe was made of four primal elements. Earth — the solid foundation of matter. Water — the fluid source of life. Fire — the power and energy of the sun. Air — the stuff you breathe. Alchemists spent centuries trying to use these elements to unlock the secrets of the universe — to turn lead into gold and create the philosopher's stone, the secret to perfection.
02
The Purpose
Alchemy for Character
This program explores the alchemy of an individual's character. It breaks down and organizes admirable qualities so that you — a young alchemist — can improve on them one by one. The idea is simple: if you break awesomeness down into its essential elements, you can more easily become Way. More. Awesome. Being awesome isn't hard to achieve. Most people squander their time and settle for mediocrity. But not you.
03
The Structure
Four Pillars, 36 Principles
The program is divided into four pillars — one for each element — each containing nine principles. Complete all six requirements for a principle and you earn an Advancement Pin. Earn all nine pins in a pillar and you qualify for that pillar's Medal. 36 principles total. There's always a next challenge.
04
Your Team
Sponsor & Companion
In an ideal scenario, every participant has a Sponsor — usually a parent — to guide and sign off on progress. You should also have a Companion of roughly the same age and skill level for encouragement and healthy competition. The Companion's Sponsor acts as Co-Sponsor, an independent gatekeeper who confirms you've truly mastered each requirement — not just memorized it for a day.

◆ The Three Tiers ◆
Awesome
Complete All 6 Requirements
The baseline. Things everyone should know or be able to do. When you earn this pin, you can confidently say you are capable in this principle — and prove it a year later.
Epic
Pick Any 3 from the List
Impressive feats above and beyond. Choose three from roughly ten challenging options per principle. These separate the determined from everyone else.
Legendary
Major Project + Write-up
Work with your Sponsor to write a proposal for a project requiring 20+ hours of preparation. Complete it, then write a 500–1,000 word summary. Must have Awesome and Epic first.
On Passing Off Requirements
The Co-Sponsor must use good judgment. The goal is genuine mastery, not one-time memorization. They should be able to repeat a requirement a month later, not just recite it once. Don't be too quick — but also respect physical, financial, or geographic limitations.
Earning a Medal
When you earn all nine Achievement Pins for a pillar, you qualify for that pillar's Medal. During the medal interview, the Co-Sponsor will review selected Awesome level requirements at random. If you can't still do them, you must re-earn that pin first.
